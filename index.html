Shared grocery list connecting...
Kitchen
Wipe down counters and stovetop after any cooking session.
Clean inside microwave and wipe exterior of all appliances.
Empty, scrub, and dry the sink.
Sweep and mop the floor.
Replace shared communal supplies as needed and square up with everyone at the end of the week.
Bathroom
Wipe down toilet seat and exterior if needed.
Wipe down sink, faucet, and mirror.
Wipe down shower or tub walls and fixtures if needed.
Sweep and mop the floor.
Restock toilet paper if running low.
Living room
Vacuum or sweep all floors including under furniture.
Wipe down coffee table, side tables, and other surfaces.
Dust shelves, TV stand, and electronics.
Straighten cushions, throws, and any shared clutter.
Trash
Empty all bins in kitchen, bathroom, and living room.
Carry bags to the building dumpster or trash area.
Reline all bins with fresh bags.
Put recycling out on collection day.
